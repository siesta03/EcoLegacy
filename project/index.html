What Is EcoLegacy

EcoLegacy is a website that highlights the importance of trees outside forests, focusing on their role in urban cooling and conservation, while encouraging user engagement in sustainability efforts.
Urban Green Infrastructure

EcoLegacy promotes the role of urban trees in cooling cities, improving air quality, and enhancing biodiversity through interactive tools and data.

Heritage and Environmental Conservation

The platform educates users about the value of preserving trees outside forests, connecting conservation efforts to cultural and natural heritage.

Community Engagemen

EcoLegacy inspires individuals, urban planners, and NGOs to participate in sustainable practices, driving positive behavior changes for a greener, more resilient future.

COOLING

The cooling effect of trees outside forests in cities helps reduce urban heat through shade and evapotranspiration. Trees lower surface and air temperatures, mitigate the urban heat island effect, improve air quality, and create comfortable, livable environments for city residents.
